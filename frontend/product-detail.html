Product Description

The iPhone 14 Pro Max is Apple's most advanced smartphone, featuring a stunning 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology for smooth scrolling and responsiveness. Powered by the A16 Bionic chip, it delivers incredible performance and efficiency for all your tasks, from gaming to video editing.

The pro camera system includes a 48MP main camera with sensor-shift optical image stabilization, 12MP Ultra Wide camera, and 12MP Telephoto camera with 3x optical zoom. It also features a new 12MP TrueDepth front camera with autofocus for sharper selfies and FaceTime calls.

With all-day battery life, Ceramic Shield front cover, and IP68 water resistance, the iPhone 14 Pro Max is built to last. It also supports 5G for faster downloads and streaming, and comes with iOS 16 pre-installed.
