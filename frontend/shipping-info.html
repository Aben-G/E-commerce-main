Shipping FAQ

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping within the US typically takes 3-5 business days after processing. International orders can take 7-21 business days depending on the destination and customs processing times.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $4.99 applies for standard shipping. Free shipping is automatically applied at checkout when your order qualifies.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

We can only change the shipping address if the order hasn't been processed for shipping yet. Please contact our customer service team immediately with your order number and the correct shipping address. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot make any guarantees once the order has entered the shipping process.

What should I do if my package is lost or stolen?

If your tracking information shows that your package was delivered but you can't find it, please check with neighbors, building management, or any safe locations where the package might have been left. If you still can't locate it, please contact us within 7 days of the delivery date, and we'll work with the carrier to investigate. For high-value items, we recommend requiring a signature upon delivery.

Do you ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses?

Yes, we can ship to PO Boxes, APO, FPO, and DPO addresses. Please note that some shipping methods and certain products may not be available for delivery to these addresses. Additionally, delivery times may be longer for military addresses. When shipping to a PO Box, please ensure the shipping method selected is USPS, as other carriers cannot deliver to PO Boxes.
